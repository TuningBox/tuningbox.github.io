Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 7, 2025

1. Introduction

TuningBox ("We", "Us", "Our", "App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use the TuningBox mobile application ("Application").

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Device Information

  • Device ID: A unique device identifier is created and stored for the app to function.
  • Device Type: Your device model, operating system version, and platform information.
  • App Usage Data: Your interactions within the app and usage statistics.

2.2. User Content

  • Photos: Car photos you upload for AI processing are temporarily processed.
  • Generated Content: Modified images and videos created by the application.

2.3. Credit System Information

  • Credit Balance: The amount of credits in your account.
  • Transaction History: Credit usage and purchase history.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To provide AI modification and video effect services.
  • Credit Management: To manage the credit system and record transactions.
  • App Improvement: To improve app performance and user experience.
  • Debugging: To detect and resolve technical issues.

4. Information Sharing

4.1. Third-Party Services

Your photos are sent to the following services for AI processing:

  • Wiro AI: Image processing and AI model services (Nano-Banana, Nano-Banana Pro, Flux 2 Pro)
  • EachLabs / Kling AI: Video generation and cinematic effects

These services have their own privacy policies that apply. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies to understand how they handle your data.

4.2. Data Retention

  • Photos: Automatically deleted from our servers after processing is complete.
  • Device ID: Stored as long as your account is active.
  • Credit Information: Stored as long as your account is active.

5. Data Security

  • All data transfers are encrypted using HTTPS protocol.
  • API keys and sensitive information are securely stored on backend servers.
  • Photos are temporarily processed and deleted after processing.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights:

  • Access Right: You can request access to your collected data.
  • Correction Right: You can request correction of incorrect or incomplete data.
  • Deletion Right: You can request deletion of your account and data.
  • Objection Right: You can object to data processing.

To exercise these rights: You can send an email to tuningbox.ai@gmail.com

7. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 years of age. If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will immediately delete that information.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our app currently does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.

9. Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes. The current version will always be published on this page.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Support Email: tuningbox.ai@gmail.com

Website: https://tuningbox.github.io

Response Time: We aim to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours.
